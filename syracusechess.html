Interested in playing chess in Syracuse?

Last updated: 10/20/2023

Syracuse doesn't have an official chess club with rated games. However, there are a large number of regular OTB meet-ups scattered around the region. This list is my attempt at gathering them all together in one place.

With the exception of the monthly High Noon tournament, most of these are located in various nearby suburbs. For those interested in USCF-rated classical games, I have provided a separate list of regional opportunities following the list of casual meet-up games.

If you're a student at Syracuse University, check out their chess club.

Regular OTB meet-ups

  • Mondays:

    • Town of Sullivan chess club (Chittenango): every Monday night, 6:30pm until 8 or so, 707 Mohawk St., Chittenango. Check out their chess.com group.

    • dsfsfs Monday: Liverpool

    • sdfsfs Monday: Brewerton

  • Wednesdays:

    • Wegmans cafe (DeWitt): every Wednesday, 6:30pm until whenever people leave.

  • Thursdays:

    • Barnes and Noble cafe (DeWitt): every Thursday afternoon, 1pm until 5pm or so.

    • sdfsfs Thursday: Baldwinsville

  • Sundays:

    • High Noon chess tournament: every third Sunday of the month at noon in the Toomey Abbott Towers in Syracuse. This is a serious but not USCF-rated tournament. 6 rounds, 25-minutes per side (no increment or delay). One dollar fee to enter. On the other Sundays, some of the players host a Lichess rapid tournament in this group.

    • Ithaca Chess Club: every Sunday from noon until 4pm, DeWitt Mall. More info on their website.
